Bio

My name is Matthew Konrad an I am Game Sound Designer. With a decade of experience in music composition and a passion for creating immersive audio experiences, I am a Game Sound Designer skilled in using industry-standard tools and technologies. Proficient in DAWs like FL Studio, Pro Tools, and Logic Pro, I bring a versatile approach to sound creation and editing. I have extensive experience working with Unity and Unreal Engine 5, utilizing Wwise to enhance audio integration and interactivity within game environments. Throughout my career, I have dedicated myself to shaping dynamic soundscapes that enrich gameplay and evoke emotion, continuously refining my craft to push the boundaries of audio in interactive media.
